About MotoMitra

India's most trusted second-hand two-wheeler inspection & valuation platform — built to eliminate fraud, hidden issues, and unfair pricing in the used bike market.

Who We Are

MotoMitra was created with one mission — to bring **transparency, trust, and peace of mind** to every two-wheeler buyer in India.

With expert mechanics, AI-powered valuation, and digital inspection reports, we ensure that every customer knows exactly what they’re paying for. No lies. No middlemen. No surprises.

Our Values

Everything we do is built on these core principles

Transparency

No hidden issues. No hidden charges. Full inspection clarity.

Accuracy

Certified mechanics and AI-driven pricing ensure honest evaluation.

Customer First

We work for buyers & sellers — not middlemen or dealers.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the used bike market with **expert inspections, digital reporting, AI valuation**, and a commitment to honesty.

We aim to make every second-hand bike purchase feel as safe as buying a brand new one.
